“广东软件科学园项目提升

汤宜飞设计作品/togihi


珠三角作为我国五大北斗产业区域之一，具有起步早、产值高、企业多、创新活力强、应用市场广阔、产业配套能力强的特点。广州在北斗芯片研发、高精度板卡和终端、以及应用解决方案上有优势， 与北京、上海并列为国家三大北斗产业园综合示范园区地区，在广州黄埔区科学城就集聚了上百家北斗上下游企业。
自成立以来，公司牢记“扶持中小企业，培育未来产业”的使命.采用“政府引导、市场化运作”的运营模式，通过建设产业园区、搭建服务平台、完善生态体系，形成了企业自我造血能力， 国有初始投资资本实现有效增值，公司可持续发展动能不断增强。
行业地位保持领先，公技术服务能力持续提升，探索新模式引领新发展项目符合广州开发区发展定位和规划布局作为广州经济发展的核心引擎，黄埔区、广州开发区 将加快构建科技创新、 数字经济带动的现代产业体系， 在全省打造新发展格局战略支点中强化“黄埔支撑”。“推动北业化应用、引领未来产业发展、激发数字经济活力”为目标的广东软件科学园建设项目能有 效促进产业资源及创新要素集聚，推进数字经济发展赋能，符合广州开发区发展战略目标。
有利于推动软件产业发展;有利于引导培育新兴产业，有利于促进创新资源聚集;有利于加速壮大区域新经济。

As one of the five Beidou industry regions in China, the Pearl River Delta is characterized by its early start, high output value, many enterprises, strong innovation, broad application market, and strong industrial support capability. Guangzhou has advantages in Beidou chip research and development, high-precision boards and terminals, and application solutions, and is one of the three major Beidou industrial park demonstration cities in China, along with Beijing and Shanghai, and hundreds of Beidou upstream and downstream enterprises have gathered in Guangzhou Huangpu Science Park.
Since its establishment, the company has kept in mind the mission of "supporting small and medium-sized enterprises and fostering future industries". By adopting the operation mode of "government-led and market-oriented operation", the company has formed the ability of self-blood generation through building industrial parks, establishing service platforms and perfecting the ecological system, and the initial state-owned investment capital has realized effective value-added and the sustainable development momentum of the company has been enhanced.
As the core engine of Guangzhou's economic development, Huangpu District and Guangzhou Development Zone will accelerate the construction of a modern industrial system driven by technological innovation and digital economy, and strengthen the "Huangpu support" in the province's strategic pivot point to build a new development pattern. "Huangpu support". The construction project of Guangdong Software Science Park, which aims to "promote the application of northern industry, lead the development of future industry and stimulate the vitality of digital economy", can effectively promote the gathering of industrial resources and innovation elements and empower the development of digital economy, which is in line with the development strategy objectives of Guangzhou Development Zone.
It is conducive to promoting the development of software industry, guiding the cultivation of new industries, promoting the gathering of innovative resources and accelerating the growth of the new regional economy.
